What is Dev & Beyond ?

Programming is an important aspect for any developer but in D&B let’s keep the code aside and talk about everything else required to be a kickass developer.

All the sessions will be held online for everyone, anywhere, and anytime to watch on some very interesting topics like productivity hacks, branding hacks, designing aspects, thinking strategies and facing interviews.

After the difficult times of COVID-19, all the sessions will be In-Person events to be held locally at Microsoft Reactor Bangalore.



Schedule

@Account

Dev & Designing

Dev & productivity

@Account

In this session we will discuss about the productivity hacks for a developer. That means how a developer can enhance their own as well as their product's productivity.

Dev & Thinking

@Account

In this session we aim to cover the topic of skillful thinking for a developer. Being a developer doesn’t only mean that you can only develop some software or any system, instead you also need to think over any problems and navigate skillfully through it.

Dev & Interview

@Account

This is the last session and through this we aim to impart our knowledge of why it is important to take some good technical interviews. This not only will boost the aspirants morale and will help them better understand the need for taking good technical interviews.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How do I get registered & get all the updates?

A. Register on the MSFT Reactor Bangalore meetup page, all the updates and reminders will sent from the Meetup platform.

Q. Is it paid/free?

A. All the sessions will be free, even all sessions will be recorded and will be made available for everyone.

Q. Will I get certificiates to attend the session?

A. There will be no certificates to attend, In-case any session requires some work to do, then after completition of work, will get you certified.

Q. Where will be sessions held?

A. All sessions will be conducted on MS Teams and no signup is required to join to the session.

Q. Is there any notification channel to join for updates?

A. We have made unofficial channels for everyone to join, we respect your privacy and other concerns, so only admins will have access to send a message in the group to prevent the unneccessary spams.
